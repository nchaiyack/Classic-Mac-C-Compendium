About Indented LDEF -	Makes a simple list with optional (fixed-width) indents:				<0 or more Tabs '\t'> <Text Data>		   (? Bytes)           (? Bytes)		    	 	 Each tab will indent 12 pixels, and you can include as many as you	 like at the front of the cell.	 	Written by - Matt Slot, fprefect@umich.edu
